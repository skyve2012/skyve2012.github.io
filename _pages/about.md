I am an Applied Scientist II at Amazon, working on agentic recommendation system design, agent fine-tuning, and large-scale low-latency recommender systems. My research interests focus on scalable personalization, representation learning, and robust ML methods that translate into real-world impact.

I received my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering (Data Science and AI) and my M.S. in Statistics from the [University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign](https://illinois.edu/). I earned my B.S. in Mathematics and Statistics from Capital Normal University.

My research spans several areas:
- **Recommendation systems** and personalization at scale
- **Agentic AI** and model fine-tuning
- **Graph neural networks** and representation learning
- **Domain generalization** and robust ML
- **Statistical inference** and feature selection (Knockoff methods)
- **Time series modeling** and signal processing (gravitational wave detection)
